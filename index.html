SUGAR BELLE & OBERON

My husband and I have two cats named named Sugar Belle and Oberon.
Sugar is a sweet little battle tabby, and Oberon is a cute ball of white fluff with different colored eyes. Both of our cats are rescues.

What's a Rescue Cat?

A rescue cat is a cat that was surrendered, or found (hence the term "rescued"). An organization will care for and foster the kitty until a forever home adopts him/her/them. Sugar Belle was rescued from a high kill shelter in Kentucky, and Oberon was found inside a tree up North.

Gentle Touch Animal Sanctuary is the organization from which we got Sugar Belle.

Feline Rescue is the organization from which we got Oberon.
