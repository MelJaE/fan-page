Sugar Belle & Oberon

My husband and I have two cats named named Sugar Belle and Oberon. Sugar is a sweet little battle tabby, and Oberon is a cute ball of white fluff with different colored eyes. Both of our cats are rescues.

What's a Rescue Cat?

A rescue cat is a cat that was surrendered, or found (hence the term "rescued").
An organization will care for and foster the kitty until a forever home adopts him/her/them.
Sugar Belle was rescued from a high kill shelter in Kentucky, and Oberon was found inside a tree up North.

Gentle Touch Animal Sanctuary is the organization from which we got Sugar Belle.

  • Gentle Touch Animal Sanctuary

Feline Rescue is the organization from which we got Oberon.

Cats Make Excellent Companions

They make a house feel more like a home.

They'll never let you forget when it's time for dinner.

Q&A

Q: How many cats does is take to become a crazy cat lady?

A: A lot. Probably more than 3. Check your City's ordinances to find out what the legal limit is.

When they're not sleeping, they like to watch birds from the window.
